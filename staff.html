Patrick Sheehan check

Patrick Sheehan is a member of the Arkansas Little Library Project.

Role

Patrick's role in the project is to find ways to contact partners, associates, hosts for our little libraries, and locations. Then come back to the team and give us the information he has found, so we can collect it, keep it, and process it. Sheehan's role is extremely important to the project, if we didn't have him we wouldn't be able to find locations, get permission, and host our little libraries.
Main Jobs Powers
Find partners and hosts for little libraries Information collection- Collect information about potential partners, locations, and associations.
Data transference Tracking
